Privacy Policy

Version 1.1, 2 April 2013

Your privacy and safety online are very important to us. To protect your privacy, we limit what we collect and what we publish on the website.

Using Catrobat Software

You do not have to tell us anything about yourself to download Catrobat application software. When you download Catrobat software or sign up to a Catrobat website, we give you the option of telling us some things about yourself, but you do not have to fill in any of this information if you want to.

Using Catroid Websites

You can try out user contributed programs on Catrobat websites without registering or providing any information. As you use the websites, we collect some data on where you click and which parts of the site you visit. This 'click data' helps us figure out ways to improve the websites. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Contacting Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please send an email to webmaster@catroid.obat.
